# Downloadable Resources

#### Shapefile with VectorNet mapping polygons (2021)
This shapefile is associated with the dataset [VectorDistributionStatus](https://www.vectornetdata.org/occurrence/search/?view=datasets&entity=d_b2390da7-9704-4215-b038-3727df6e7fee).

The mapping polygons are ‘regional’ administrative units are NUTS3 from Eurostat/GISCO or equivalent, NUTS2 or equivalent (in Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Germany, The Netherlands, England (UK), Scotland (UK) and Wales (UK)), or Global Administrative Unit Layers (GAUL) dataset of FAO (northern part of Sweden and outside EU/EEA and Western Balkans and Türkiye). The NUTS system used is that of [2021 version](https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/web/nuts/history), and the GAUL system is the 2015 edition. Administrative boundaries: ©EuroGeographics ©UN-FAO ©Turkstat.
