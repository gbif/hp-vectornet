# Downloadable Resources

#### Shapefile with VectorNet mapping polygons (2021)
This shapefile is associated with the dataset [VectorDistributionStatus](https://www.vectornetdata.org/occurrence/search/?view=datasets&entity=d_b2390da7-9704-4215-b038-3727df6e7fee).

**Mapping polygons correspond to 'regional' administrative units:**
- **NUTS 3** (Eurostat/GISCO or equivalent)
- **NUTS 2 or equivalent** in Austria, Belgium, Denmark, Germany, the Netherlands, England (UK), Scotland (UK), and Wales (UK)
- **Global Administrative Unit Layers (GAUL) from FAO** for northern Sweden and regions outside the EU/EEA, the Western Balkans, and Türkiye

The NUTS system used is the [2021 version](https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/web/nuts/history); the GAUL system is the 2015 edition.
